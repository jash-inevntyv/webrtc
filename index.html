Hosting

AWS
  • Scalability and Reliability: Easily handle large user bases and peak traffic with AWS's robust infrastructure and global reach.
  • Comprehensive Services: Leverage a wide range of cloud services, including compute, storage, networking, and media services, to complement your WebRTC app.
  • Pay-as-You-Go Model: Optimize costs by paying only for the resources you use.
  • AWS was first market mover
  • Connectivity between different services and tools are simpler
